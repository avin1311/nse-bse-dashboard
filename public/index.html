Positions

Paper positions only — nothing here places a real order. While this tab is open, targets/stop-losses are checked every few seconds and trigger the banner at the top of the page. They're also checked server-side on a schedule (independent of your browser) and sent to Telegram if configured — see the README for setup. Each position also gets an automatic one-time deviation warning (amber ⚠️) at the halfway point between entry and stop-loss, as an early heads-up before the hard stop actually hits.
